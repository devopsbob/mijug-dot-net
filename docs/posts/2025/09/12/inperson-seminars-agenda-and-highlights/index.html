Workshop Preview: 90-Minute Linux Transformation Journey

Published Sep 12, 2025 • Updated Sep 12, 2025 • MIJUG Team

Inside look at our hands-on Linux workshops - from Windows 11 compatibility checks to essential backup skills in just 90 minutes!

#seminar#linux#windows-11#workshop-preview#hands-on#education#agenda

🎬 What Makes These Workshops Special?

With just days left until our September seminars begin, we’re excited to give you a sneak peek at the carefully crafted 90-minute journey that’s already helping attendees save $200-800 on unnecessary computer purchases!

⏱️ Your 90-Minute Transformation Journey

👋 Welcome & Setup (10 minutes)

We start by getting everyone comfortable and connected. No intimidating tech jargon - just friendly introductions and making sure you’re ready for the hands-on experience ahead.

⚠️ Windows 10 Deadline Reality Check (10 minutes)

October 14, 2025 is approaching fast! We’ll show you exactly what the Windows 10 end-of-support means for your daily computer use and why this deadline affects millions of perfectly good computers.

🗺️ What We’ll Cover Together (5 minutes)

A quick roadmap of your personalized learning journey - so you know exactly what skills you’ll gain and how they’ll help you make confident decisions about your computer’s future.

🖥️ Check Your Computer’s Future (10 minutes)

The moment of truth! Using simple, step-by-step guidance, you’ll discover whether your current computer can run Windows 11 - or if it’s a perfect candidate for a Linux transformation.

🛠️ Hands-On Skills That Matter (35 minutes)

This is where the magic happens! Our interactive demonstration covers:

  • 🔐 Backup & Security Mastery: Learn to protect your important files and understand what sensitive data might be hiding on old machines
  • 🐧 Linux in Action: See a real Linux desktop in action - it’s more familiar than you think!
  • 💾 USB Backup Strategies: Master the art of organizing and securing your digital life on portable storage
  • 🔧 Simple Troubleshooting: Gain confidence handling common computer challenges

☕ Break & Questions (10 minutes)

Stretch your legs, grab some refreshments, and get personalized answers to your specific computer questions. This is where individual concerns get addressed!

🎯 Your Personal Action Plan (10 minutes)

No one leaves empty-handed! You’ll walk away with a clear, personalized roadmap for your next steps - whether that’s Windows 11 upgrade, Linux conversion, or strategic computer replacement.

🌟 Real Value, Real Results

💰 Expected Savings: $200-800 per household
🎯 Success Rate: Attendees leave with actionable plans, not confusion
🤝 Support Level: Continued guidance through our website and resources

🎯 Perfect For You If…

  • ✅ You have old PCs gathering dust with unknown sensitive data
  • ✅ You want to repurpose machines for streaming, gaming, or projects
  • ✅ You prefer USB backups over keeping outdated systems
  • ✅ You’re curious about Linux but don’t know where to start
  • ✅ You’d rather save money than buy new hardware

🍎 Mac Users Welcome Too!

While macOS isn’t Linux, it shares UNIX roots. We’ll explore directory structures and Terminal commands, with bonus tips for modern Intel and Apple Silicon Macs.

👨‍🏫 Your Guide: Real Teaching Experience

Your presenter brings:

  • 🎓 University Teaching experience at Wayne State University in Detroit
  • 👥 Technology User Group leadership focused on community learning
  • 💡 Adult Education expertise, especially helping career changers gain tech confidence

  • 📖 Easy-to-follow guides and reference materials
  • 🤝 Continued support for your Linux adventure

  • 🌐 Transform unused hardware into a web browsing, email, and streaming powerhouse
  • 👴👵 Create safe, simple systems perfect for family use
  • 💰 Avoid unnecessary expenses while staying digitally connected
