Computer Assessment and Guidance Services

Do you have an older computer and aren’t sure what to do with it? Just like your refrigerator, washing machine, or car, computers are appliances that need occasional service and maintenance to keep running well. We provide consultation services to help you understand your options and make informed decisions about your technology.

What We Offer

Computer Assessment - For a consultation fee, we’ll evaluate your older computer to determine its capabilities and potential uses - just like having a technician assess whether your appliance is worth repairing.

Backup Services - We can help you safely backup important files, photos, and documents before making any changes to your system.

Repurposing Guidance - Learn about possibilities for giving your computer new life, whether for basic internet use, family communication, or simple tasks.

Safe Disposal Planning - Get advice on how to properly wipe personal data and safely dispose of or donate your computer if repurposing isn’t viable.

Educational Support - Patient, one-on-one consultation to help you understand your options without pressure to make immediate decisions. Think of us as your computer’s “service technician” who explains things in plain language.

Our Consultation Process:

  • Assessment of your current computer’s condition and capabilities
  • Discussion of your needs and technical comfort level
  • Exploration of repurposing options that might work for you
  • Guidance on data backup and security considerations
  • Information about safe disposal or donation if replacement is preferred

Our goal is to provide honest, helpful information so you can make the best decision for your situation - whether that’s repurposing, replacing, or responsibly disposing of your older computer.
